Interests

When I'm not coding or aggressively loving my pets, I am planning my next trip. Whether it be a weekend away in the hill country sipping wine or disovering french pop bands in New Orleans. There's nothing I enjoy more than experiencing a new environment or culture. Most of my trips focus on the food; I am on a hunt to find those hidden restaurants that the locals try to keep secret.

Education

I have recently completed the Data Analysis and Visualization bootcamp at the University of Texas in Austin. During this time I dove deep into python programming and web development; I quickly realized my passion and am so excited to start a career where I can combine logic and design. My previous study in Electrical Engineering helped me realize my craving for creativeness and something more personable.

Experience

After completing the bootcamp and regaining my sanity, I was eager for more. I became addicted to overcoming the challenge of learning a new skill and having that "AH HA!!" moment. I began to advertise myself as a freelance web developer and was able build up my own clientele. For every new client I challenge myself by obtaining a new skill and sharpening my previous ones.
